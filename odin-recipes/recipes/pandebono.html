Pan de Bono

Home

Pan de bono is a soft, cheesy bread made with cassava starch and fresh cheese

crispy outside, chewy inside. Usually enjoyed warm with coffee or hot chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cassava starch (yuca starch or tapioca flour)
  • 1 cup grated queso fresco or queso campesino
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix cassava starch, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Add both cheeses and mix well.
  4. Add the egg and milk gradually until you form a soft dough that doesn’t stick to your hands.
  5. Shape small balls (like golf balls).
  6. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 15–20 minutes until golden.
  7. Serve warm — they’re best eaten fresh out of the oven!
